What Makes Us Unique?

An Agricultural Setting

Located on 5 beautiful acres in Grovetown behind our home, your pets can relax in nature.

VIPs only

We board only a minimal number of pets at a time. So not only do your pets have their own indoor/outdoor space they have more undivided attention.

The "Good Dog" Policy

All outdoor runs are separated; so no fence fighting. For safety, all play time is monitored and no pet is ever left alone unattended.

Environment

Your pet will stay in a comfortable, climate controlled room with full-time access to their own personal outdoor space.

Competitive Pricing

We charge by the night rather than the day, saving you money.

Photo Updates

If you're curious about how your pet is doing without you we even take the time to send a daily picture or two of your pets activities.
